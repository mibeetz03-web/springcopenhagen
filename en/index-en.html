Refinement

The Christel series includes twelve artfully designed figures made of fine bisque porcelain, shaped by a keen eye for detail and a timeless, stylish aesthetic.

A design that translates the symbolism of the stars into tangible beauty. The figures make a special gift, a personal object with symbolic meaning or a stylish decorative piece with character.

Christel Marott

Each figure bears the signature style of Danish artist Christel Marott (1919–1992), combining artistic refinement with the deeper meaning of the zodiac signs.

Soft forms and a matte surface give the objects a calm, almost mystical presence.
